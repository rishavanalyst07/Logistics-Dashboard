# Logistics-Dashboard

Description - Developed a Power BI dashboard to monitor Kraasa logistics KPIs including TAT, SLA, deliveries, and RTO. Provided clear order lifecycle tracking across each stage and highlighted operational delays. Empowered business teams with real-time insights to reduce delays and improve delivery efficiency.

1. Built dynamic date-range analytics to compare order trends with previous rolling periods, helping identify volume spikes and process bottlenecks.
2. Implemented key logistics KPIs—Delivered, Pending, In-Transit, Cancelled, RTO, Delivered Within TAT—giving management instant visibility into order health.
3. Developed TAT visuals (O2D, O2S, S2D) to show average stage-wise timelines and help operations pinpoint delays in the logistics chain.
4. Built RTO Split analysis (Today, 7-day, 14-day) to identify failed delivery patterns and support better logistics planning.
5. Designed SLA tracking for Closed and Open cases to show TAT compliance, highlight breach risks, and support proactive coordination with courier partners.
6. Enhanced decision-making by providing end-to-end visibility across the logistics pipeline, reducing manual tracking and enabling faster intervention.
7. Helped reduce SLA breaches and improve customer satisfaction by enabling teams to monitor TAT deviations and act on at-risk shipments early.
